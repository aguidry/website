My name is Alicia Guidry. I work as the Medicaid Health Information Technology Coordinator for the Informatics Research Institute at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette on a contract with the Louisiana Department of Health .


I received my B.S. in Computer Science with a concentration in Information Systems and Decision Sciences from Louisiana State University and my Ph.D. in Biomedical and Health Informatics at the University of Washington. I was advised by James F. Brinkley. My dissertation research focuses on the use of data integration and ontologies to provide access to information using the open source software OpenClinica and OpenMRS.


My curriculum vitae summarizes my academic and professional history. For more information, I can be reached via email at aguidry [at] gmail [dot] com.
